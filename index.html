4-Layer Security

Built for enforcement.
Not hope.

We lend to US corporations owning Caribbean hospitality assets. Every loan is structured for maximum investor protection with four independent layers of collateral — and a full in-house team to enforce them instantly.

Layer 01
1st & 2nd Position Mortgage

Registered directly against the Caribbean hospitality asset. Priority lien — we are first in line at any sale or foreclosure.

Layer 02
Corporate Share Pledge

Borrower pledges shares of the US corporation that owns the asset. Default triggers immediate transfer of ownership — no court delays.

Layer 03
Personal Guarantee

Individual principals personally guarantee the loan. Full recourse against the borrower beyond the corporate structure.

Layer 04
North American Asset Cross-Collateral

US and Canadian borrowers pledge North American assets as additional security — legally enforceable in their domestic jurisdiction.

Tax & Structure

Four paths to
tax-advantaged returns.

📉
100% Bonus Depreciation

Qualifying hospitality assets — furniture, fixtures, equipment, and short-life personal property — may qualify for 100% bonus depreciation in the year of acquisition. CIF's in-house advisory team identifies and maximizes depreciation opportunities for investors in qualifying projects.

🔄
1031 Exchange Opportunities

CIF structures 1031-eligible individual asset co-investments, allowing investors to defer capital gains from property sales into Caribbean hospitality assets. Pre-construction discounts and exchange timelines are designed with 1031 compliance in mind.

🏦
Self-Directed IRA

Invest your retirement savings and earn 12% tax-deferred (Traditional IRA) or tax-free (Roth IRA). CIF works with leading custodians including Equity Trust, Alto IRA, and Entrust Group. Minimum $100,000 — your IRA earns $1,000/month in the Caribbean.

📈
Solo & Self-Directed 401(k)

Roll an existing 401(k) into a self-directed plan and deploy capital directly into CIF. Business owners and self-employed investors can use a Solo 401(k) for higher contribution limits and direct investment control. Partner advisory services included for setup and compliance.
